## Accelerating discovery in India through AI-powered science and education
- **URL:** https://deepmind.google/blog/accelerating-discovery-in-india-through-ai-powered-science-and-education/
- **Published:** 2026-02-18
- **Description:** Google DeepMind announced a National Partnership for AI with Indian government bodies focusing on scientific research, education, and sectoral applications. The partnership provides access to frontier AI models like AlphaGenome and AI Co-scientist to Indian researchers, integrates Gemini into educational programs serving 11 million students, and collaborates on agricultural resilience and renewable energy forecasting.
