## Anthropic partners with the UK Government to bring AI assistance to GOV.UK services
- url: https://www.anthropic.com/news/gov-UK-partnership
- published: 2026-01-27
- description: Anthropic has been selected by the UK's Department for Science, Innovation and Technology to build and pilot an AI-powered assistant for GOV.UK. The system, powered by Claude, will help citizens navigate government services with personalized guidance, initially focusing on employment support.
- relevance: Demonstrates Claude's real-world deployment in agentic systems that go beyond question-answering to guide users through complex processes. Highlights Claude's capabilities in government AI applications and the importance of safety and user control in production systems.
